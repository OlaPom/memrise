Get started (web)

Create an account and choose your first course

To use Memrise, you must register. You can create a new account using your email address or sign up with your Google, Facebook, or Apple account.

  1. Go to www.memrise.com and click Get started.
  2. Choose the language you want to learn.
  3. Enter your email address and password and click Register.
  4. Choose your course level.
You have now opened the dashboard.
Tip: The number of available courses depends on the language in the "I speak" field. It is filled in depending on the language of your device, but you can change it at any time. If you select English in the "I speak" field, you'll have access to courses in 23 languages. If you select Polish, you will only see 5 languages to choose from.
