Get started (mobile)

Install the mobile app and register with Memrise

The app requires Android 8.0 or higher and iOS 15.0 or higher.

  1. Install the application on your device:



  2. Open the app and tap Get started
  3. Choose your language in the "I speak" field (filled automatically based on the language of your device).
  4. Tap the language you want to learn.
  5. Sign up with your email:
    1. Enter your email and password.
    2. Tap Create account.
    You can also sign up with your Facebook, Google, or Apple account.
  6. Choose your course level.
