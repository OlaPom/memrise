Communicate

Use AI to boost your language skills

This module is the most interactive. You can use your knowledge in a simulated conversation with an AI-powered chatbot (MemBot).

You can practice two types of situations:
  • Chats - general conversations, for example, about your favorite music
  • Missions - situations where you need to reach a goal, for example, order a coffee

With speech recognition, you can turn the chat into a voice conversation and practice speaking.

If at any point you feel stuck, MemBot can help you:
  • translate a sentence to your language.
  • with a hint if you don't know how to answer.
MemBot also corrects any spelling error you may make.
Note: In the free version, you can have two conversations per day. You can watch an ad to unlock an additional one.
